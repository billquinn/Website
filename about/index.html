About Me.

I am a doctoral candidate in the English Department at Northeastern University. My research interests include computational text analysis, periodical studies, and American literature. My work examines the readers of magazines who actively participated in the construction of literary taste. By looking at letters to the editors, I consider the readers’ interpretations and reception of modernity and cultural works, as well as explore a prevalent but under-researched communication system that shaped modes of literary production. I employ digital methods to supplement my close readings of letters and modernist literature in order to track literary influence over time.
